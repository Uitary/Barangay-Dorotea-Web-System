Our Story

A Brief History of Barangay 99

Barangay 99, Zone 9, District II is one of the vibrant communities of Caloocan City. Known locally as the Dorotea community, it has grown over the decades into a close-knit neighborhood committed to public service and community welfare.

Under the leadership of Punong Barangay Danilo C. Caldo and the dedicated Barangay Council, Barangay 99 continues to uphold the values of service, unity, and progress — ensuring every resident has access to the support and services they deserve.

This digital portal is part of the barangay's modernization initiative — bringing governance closer to every household in Zone 9.
